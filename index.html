About Our Practice

The type of dental care you deserve

Dr. Kapadia offers comprehensive general, cosmetic and restorative dental services for patients of ALL ages. New patients are welcome, including those who have been away from the dentist due to fear and dental anxiety.

We perform a thorough evaluation of your dental health, including the teeth, gums and jaw joints. This holistic approach is designed to identify any underlying imbalances in the bite that are the cause of common dental problems.
